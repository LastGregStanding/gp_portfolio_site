About Me

I'm a web developer with a background that blends logic and creativity. I grew up as a musician, and that creative foundation eventually led me to earn a degree in Philosophy. It was during that time—while taking a Logic course—that I discovered my passion for programming. Now, I'm finishing up a second bachelors degree in Computer Science at Oregon State University.

I have experience developing full-stack projects using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and MySQL.I enjoy both front-end and back-end work, but what drives me most is the opportunity to solve challenging problems and to keep learning new technologies.

Outside of tech, you can find me either playing guitar, experimenting in the kitchen, reading a Stephen King book, or spending time with friends.
